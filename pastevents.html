PAST EVENTS

A List of Past Events from Calder Valley FTA

PUNKS AGAINST HUNTS 2

Our 2nd fundraiser for Calder Valley Hunt Saboteurs.

We had the best line up ever! The anarcho-punk legends Icons of Filth played for us alongside Distort, Geväl and Spillage at the Blind Pig.

Thank you to the crowd for being absolutely mental too, it was a cherry on top of how great the rest of the night was!

Thanks to some very generous donations from local businesses, we managed to make £930, every penny going to the sabs. We can’t wait for the next one.

HUNT SAB WORKSHOP

UNDECLARED September 9th, 2023

An evening divulging some of the basics about what being a sab is and what it looks like on a typical day out in the field with the aim of recruiting new members to join the amazing Calder valley hunt sab team! this was a great way to meet people interested in animal rights and direct action.

CULTURE SHOCK

The Blind Pig Sowerby Bridge, September 2nd, 2023

An event fully organised by our group with the intention of fundraising and having our first public stall to get our name and what we were doing out to all of you. A very fun night spreading the message of animal rights while jamming out to classic punk.

PUNKS AGAINST HUNTS

The Blind Pig Sowerby Bridge, February 25th, 2023

This was our first event!

We were lucky enough to have the amazing Bruise Control, Meel Deel, Taurine and the Hate play for us at the Blind Pig. It was so nerve wracking leading up to it but everything went to plan!

Thank you all for your support, we couldn’t have done it without you! We raised £988 for the Calder Valley Hunt Saboteurs, what a night!.
